Consumer: A person who buys goods or avails services for consideration, including online transactions, but not for resale or commercial purposes.
Complaint: Any written allegation regarding:
Defective goods
Deficiency in services
Unfair trade practices
Overcharging
Hazardous goods/services
Defect: Any fault or shortcoming in quality, quantity, purity, or standard of goods.
Deficiency: Any inadequacy or negligence in services.
Unfair Trade Practice: False representation, misleading advertisement, refusal to refund defective goods, not issuing bills, etc.
E-commerce: Buying or selling goods or services over a digital or electronic network.
